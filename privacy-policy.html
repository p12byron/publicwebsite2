Privacy Policy for A Mazing Tower Defence

Last updated: November 2025

Overview

A Mazing Tower Defence ("the Game") is developed by PSID Apps. This privacy policy explains how information is collected and used when you play the Game.

Information We Collect

The Game itself does not directly collect personally identifiable information (PII). However, the Game uses third-party services that may collect certain data to provide their functionality.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data

Firebase (Google)

Used for:

  • Firebase Authentication – to manage user accounts and cloud saves
  • Firebase Analytics – to understand how players use the Game
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to identify and fix crashes

These services may collect device information, usage data, and crash logs.

  • Firebase Privacy Policy

Google Play Games Services

Used for authentication and sign-in functionality. May collect your Google account information and gameplay data.

  • Google Play Privacy Policy

Unity

The Game is built with Unity, which may collect analytics and diagnostic data.

  • Unity Privacy Policy

Data Usage

Data collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Display advertisements
  • Save and sync game progress across devices
  • Analyse gameplay to improve the Game
  • Identify and fix technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is only shared with the third-party service providers listed above as necessary for them to provide their services.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can reset your advertising identifier through your device settings
  • You can opt out of personalised ads through your device's ad settings
  • You can delete your cloud save data by contacting us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this policy.
